About Us

Theres a lot written about immortal robot bodies and how theyre the new rage. Religion cant provide immortal robot bodies. Flying heads in bubbles are also becoming popular. The cost of preserving a head is much less than the entire body and at least you can keep your face. But with the immortal robot, after you die, your soul or spirit is digitized and your flesh body rots, but you dont care, cause having an immortal robot body is ultra cool and sexy. While youre still on earth, your immortal robot body sits in storage ready for the fateful day. No, it doesnt act like Dorian Gray, keeping you ageless and that kind of thing, but it does provide a great feeling of peace of mind.
